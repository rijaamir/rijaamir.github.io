About Us



We welcome learners of all ages, from school students and teachers to families and community groups. Our collection – filled with treasures including ancient manuscripts, world maps, historic newspapers, music recordings, and contemporary photographs – is at the heart of our unique learning programme which offers a range of opportunities to explore, connect and interact both onsite, online and nationally.

  2. Tutoring
  3. Daycare Services
