Borrowing & Fees

How do I renew items?

To renew you can do the following:

  • Go To My Account and select Renew
  • Call or visit any library location
  • Use our Mobile App

How can I pay my fines or fees?

  1. In person– Pay by cash, check or credit card at the library during regular operating hours.
  2. Online – Log into My Account and use your credit card to pay online.

What if I do not return an item on time?

Maximum fine per overdue item is $20 or replacement cost of item, whichever is less. Items that are over a month overdue will be billed.
