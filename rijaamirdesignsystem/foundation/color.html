Color

The design system uses simple and minimalist color scheme with an inclusion of dark pink that reflects the identity of my portfolio brand. There are a total of six colors used in this system.

White

HEX Code: #FFFFFF

This color is used for the background of the entire portfolio site.


Cream White 1

HEX Code: #FFFAF4

This color is used for button's text and background on hover state. It is also used as a background color for different sections on case study detail page.


Cream White 2

HEX Code: #FDF1E4

This color is used in the logo and also for the links on navbar and for text in the footer.


Gray 1

HEX Code: #676767

This color is used for body text on the page or on the cards.


Gray 2

HEX Code: #707070

This color is used for level 3 and 4 headings.


Pink

HEX Code: #D16F8B

This color is used in the logo, buttons, favicon, and as a background for navbar and footer. Also, it also used for level 1 and level 2 headings.
