Typography

Bitter and Open Sans

The typography used in this design system helps in achieving simplicity and adds life to the elements. Bitter typeface evokes creativity due to its serif characteristics and Open Sans complements Bitter as a sans-serif typeface, perfect for accessible body text. This typography hierarchy uses the Perfect Fourth Scale with a base font size of 16px.
