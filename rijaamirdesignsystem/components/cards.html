Cards

Cards are to be used as preview of works on the landing page/homepage. The cards should be carefully laid out and should highlight important information.

Example

This example of card is responsive. Adjust the screen size to demonstrate the responsive behaviors.

DESCRIPTION

Project Title

This is a wider card with supporting text below as a natural lead-in to additional content. This content is a little bit longer.

Accessibility

Ensure that they are large enough to hold legible text for readers.

Usage

The project card is availble to highlight a shortened list of information. Use project cards to give users a highlited list of important information like project details, and process details.
