Rija Aamir Design System

Open-minded, Creative, Analytical, Approachable.

This website presents the overall style, brand, page layout, and major components used on my portfolio website. I am creative, friendly and technical and this design system website is meant to depict all these qualities of mine. I like simple and minimalistic design and this system incorporates a modern touch to design as well.

This design system uses the Bootstrap 5 framework and is divided into three sections:

FOUNDATIONS

The foundations section includes the typography, color, and logos.

COMPONENTS

The components section includes the header/navigation, buttons, cards and the process steps.

PATTERNS

The page patterns section includes the pattern for my portfolio's home page and the pattern for the case study page.
