UX DESIGN

DocChat

OVERVIEW

Our team was tasked to brainstorm ideas for a mobile and tablet application that we will be working on throughout the quarter. After a discussion with the team, we decided to put together a concept that allows patients to connect with a healthcare professional at their own convenience. This concept evolved further as we started with background research, sketched our ideas, and created moodboard for our brand. Our main assumption before we developed our concept was that people find it hard to connect with doctors specifically when they are traveling and have a medical need.

MY PROCESS

Our team was followed a framework that helped us accomplish the end product with ease.

  • Research
  • Define
  • Develop
  • Deliver

RESEARCH

We conducted competitive research, where we analyzed two existing apps in the market that provided access to doctors from anywhere to the patient. However, they have different purposes and focus on a very narrow range of needs such mental health.

DEFINE

As a team we conducted a design charette session and brainstormed our different ideas for the application. We also created a mood board for the overall design inspiration.
